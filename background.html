Background

Our background started in high voltage electrical solutions working on some of the biggest projects in the UK. Working on these projects ensured we worked to the highest possible standard and in doing so gained a sterling reputation for professionalism and quality of work. While doing these large electrical installs we tackled challenges such as drainage, humidity and making ground up.

We then expanded into bus stop replacement for the Thames Link Transport. This was replacing the old bus stops with a new bus stop produced by an engineering company in London which had advanced advertising capability. We now have contacts within this field and can offer this service across the UK. However, as a company based in South Nottinghamshire, we would like to offer this service in a local area.

The bus stops could be a massive revenue creator for the authority from local business. With the stops offering electronic adverts there is nearly limitless storage so a village bus stop could hold every village business along with surrounding businesses from other villages on the route. Not only does this service increase business in the local area but gives any village a smart and professional look.

We did not stop there, after bus stops, we expanded into play equipment and general street furniture, benches and shelters. We offer all the services with a power supply for lighting with renewable energy options. We intend to offer a full turnkey solution to any project. You will be safe in our hands and our extended warranties will ensure your peace of mind, backed up with our 24-7 call out service.

We would relish the opportunity for a presentation to showcase our services to not only increase revenue for the authority but also provide a cost-effective solution to current requirements with pipeline projects that require street works.
