BUILDING FROM THE GROUND UP

We offer complete turnkey solutions to bespoke comercial and domestic buiding along side a building maintenance divsion helping to manage over 5000 properties.

We specialise in all roofing soluttions weather it be leak, collapse or install.

We cover all aspects of the build; Ground works, Utilities, Brick work and Steel Erection, Electrics First and Second Fix, Joinery, Plastering and Painting and Decorating.

Introduction

Hedwell Construction was developed to make construction easy. We have four main divisions of the business, Ground and Utility Works, Building (Joinery, Brickwork and Plastering), Electrical Division and Roofing Division.

Our focus is on safety and realiability.

We consistently deliver projects on time and under budget

Recent scopes of work

  • Property conversion from a hotel to nine apartments – this was stripping the hotel out to an empty shell then rebuilding, wiring, heating and plastering.
  • Building strip out and office conversion.
  • Building demolishment and ground works for new housing.
  • Two story extension including the management of utility relocation, heating systems, house rewire, roofing, windows, fascia, soffits and cladding.
